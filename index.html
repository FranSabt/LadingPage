About us

MAKE MUSIC LIKE A GOD

We have a Pensum: Free, Open Source and in constant growth so that you can choose your own path in music.

How does it work?

Our curriculum is based on the experiences of numerous music schools throughout the world.
We look for the most practical, simple, and complete techniques and methods that help you to train the skills necessary to play any instrument by yourself.

Our Goals

  • Read Music
  • Play an instrument

    • We want you to be able to learn music by yourself.
    That can read sheet music and play an instrument professionally using our study materials while being supported and at the same time supporting our learning community.
